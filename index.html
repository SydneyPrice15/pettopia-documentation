User analysis

The targeted user we will want to seek interest from are the following

    1. Pet Owners
    2. Vets
    3. Pet focused N.G.O

Pet owners are people who already have a pet a may want another one and my also want to purchase food and items for the pets they already have They maybe an individual or a family. Individual owners maybe young and in their prime and will be using our site to find an animal companion.

Vets are licensed medics for animals and will mostly use the website for findings medicine and equipment for pet care , they may be the category of users that may use the site to purchase items in bulk or stocks and also make appointments with client’s pet.

Pet Focused N.G.O like "P.E.T.A" are huge independent bodies that have huge interest in the topic of animal care and relations. they may use the site to help push pet adoption rate and also be the center for trade on the website since it is an organization they may have people of all kinds and age groups.

Task analysis

Purchase of medical supplies for pets

    1. The user after successfully accessing the website home page , the user must click on the vet section and scroll through for the desired medicine

    2. If not found user should click on the search tool and type in the name of the desired medicine.

        3. The user click on the item and the gets more information on it such as producers and suppliers details.

      This process should have a duration time that will vary depending on how wide our database is for medication but shouldn’t take more than 5 minutes if we do have the desired item.

      The Adoption of pets.

        1. After successfully accessing the webpage, the user must click on the on the adopt tab and then proceed in scrolling through the profile of the pet.

        2. If the desired pet is found, the shop name and contact is displayed for the user and user for there will deal with the third party.

      This process time duration varies due to preference of the user but should be done under 30 minutes maximum.

      Make an appointment with Veterinary surgeon

        1. The user after successfully accessing the website home page , vet section and find a Vet of the users preference.

        2. If particular on is needed user should make use of the search function.

        3. Once found user can either contact the Vet through 3rd party means or ping the vet to draw his or her attention.

      This process duration time should probably take about 5 to 10 minutes.
